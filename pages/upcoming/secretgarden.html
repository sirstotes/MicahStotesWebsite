The Secret Garden

The UVU Department of Theatre presents The Secret Garden, the classic non-musical play—a heartwarming story of growth, healing, and the human spirit. Guided by a cast of memorable characters and the magic of a hidden garden, Mary and Colin discover that selflessness and connection can open the door to happiness, creating a theatrical experience full of wonder and charm.

Director: Roger Sorensen

Dates: April 3 2026 - April 11 2026
